Results and Discussions: Craters are a topographical landmark or tectonic units on the planetary surfaces. The spatial distribution and the number of craters in different planets are different, thus there are various DBs and DIs in lunar and martian surfaces. Here the craters' information are downloaded from the lunar crater database [6] and martian crater database [7].
